1 out of every 20 girls you know have been sexually assaulted in the USA

In spite of that, women do not cower in fear. We are already out there kicking ass - building careers, raising amazing children and traveling the world. But none of us have escaped this nagging feeling that follows us when we're walking alone at night with men yelling "Hey pretty lady!".

What if the world had a safety rating for every street?

Safer Streets is a movement for women to reclaim the streets and the cities that we live in - and it all begins with a single alert. If you're in a dodgy area, Safer Streets will let you know so you can take control of your own wellbeing. The Safer Streets technology analyzes crime and safety related information daily to help you make informed decisions about your wellbeing.

Built By Women, For Women

As women, we've all been in the same situation before - vulnerable and unsure of our safety. Safer Streets is a labor of love, built by a team who believes that technology has been underutilized in helping women feel safer. We hope to change that. We believe that by arming women with clarity, they can make more informed decisions about their safety and wellbeing.

Removing fear of crime, reducing vulnerability is not something that we can achieve overnight. But this is a start.
